A simple, minimalist notepad for creating and organizing your notes. Save notes locally, organize them into folders, and share them with others.

## Getting Started

Open the app in your browser. Your notes are automatically saved to your browser and will be there when you return.

## Creating Notes

1. Click the **[NEW]** button or type in the editor to start a new note
2. Give your note a title at the top
3. Write your content in the text area
4. Click **[SAVE]** or press Ctrl+S to save

## Organizing Notes

- Create **folders** to organize your notes by topic
- Create **files** inside folders to keep related notes together
- Click on any folder to open it and see its files
- Click on any file to open and edit it

## Text Formatting

Select text and use the toolbar buttons:
- **B** - Bold
- **I** - Italic
- **S** - Strikethrough
- **U** - Dotted underline

Keyboard shortcuts:
- Ctrl+B - Bold
- Ctrl+I - Italic
- Ctrl+S - Save

## Sharing Notes

1. Open the note you want to share
2. Click **[SHARE]**
3. Copy the link to share with others

Anyone with the link can view your note in their browser.

## Themes

Choose your preferred look from the theme dropdown:
- Dark - Black and grey
- Light - White and yellow
- Eclipse - IDE-style dark
- Monokai - Classic syntax colors
- Midnight - Deep blue

Your theme choice is saved and remembered.

## Help

Click **[HELP]** at the top to see this guide anytime.
